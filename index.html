OUR STORY

Classic Design.
Nordic Roots.

Our name, Leon & Noel, is a palindrome, reading the same forwards and backwards, a fitting symbol of the harmony and precision that guided our approach to watch design.

We will admit, our story followed a familiar path:
Swiss movement? Check.
Minimalist design? Check.
A decade in the making? Surprisingly, check.
Great quality at a fair price? Absolutely.

But here is the thing. Our watches were never meant to rely on a story alone. You did not always need a narrative to fall in love with a timepiece. Sometimes, design, craftsmanship, and attention to detail said everything that needed to be said.

Time will tell. Tell with time.

Sparkling 36

Bubbly Drinks &
Sun-Drenched Landscapes

Sparkling 36 is an homage to bubbly drinks and sun-drenched landscapes. It has a beige dial with bordeaux hour marks, framed by a ribbed bezel and brushed rose-gold case. Produced in a limited batch of 100 pcs.

Prism 36

Our Personal Favourite

Prism 36 is our personal favourite from the collection. The dial is encapsulated by a brushed case with a smooth bezel. Produced in a limited batch of 100 pcs.

Sundown 36

The Most Classic Watch

Sundown 36 is perhaps the most classic watch from the collection. The deep blue dial is encapsulated by a brushed rose-gold case with a smooth bezel. Produced in a limited batch of 100 pcs.

Contest 36

High Up on the Pedestal

High up on the pedestal you will find Contest 36. The rich racing-green sun-ray dial is framed by a ribbed bezel on a brushed silver case. Produced in a limited batch of 100 pcs.
